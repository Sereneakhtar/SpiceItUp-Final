Featured Recipe: Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp of oil or to your preference
  • 1/4 cup diced carrot
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 tbsp of diced garlic
  • 1/2 diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cups peas
  • 1/8 cup corn
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp of seasme oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

Step 1: Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. add carrot, onion, and garlic. Cook until onion is translucent. Step 2: Add bell peppers and broccoli. Cook for 3-5 minutes until tender. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan. Step 3: Pour beaten eggs into the empty side of the pan. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix them with the vegetables. Step 4: Add peas, corn, rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil salt and pepper. Mix well. Let rice cook till a little crispy. until heated through. Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy.

3-Ingredient Teriyaki Chicken

Serving Size: 4 people Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs of skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cubed.
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce (liquid aminos can be substituted)
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar.

    • Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 15 min Total Time: 30 min Instructions:

  • 1. Heat a large nonstick pan overn medium-high heat. Add the chicken and sear until golden brown on both sides.
  • 2. Add the soy sauce and brown sugar and stir to combine. Bring to a boil. Cook until the sauce coats the chicken and the sauce reduces.
  • 3. Serve with rice and/or vegetables.

    • One-Pot Cheeseburger Pasta

    Serving Size: 6 people Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 cup onion diced
  • 1 tbsp of chopped garlic or garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp of worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp of ketchup
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 16 oz of fusilli pasta
  • 3 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk

    • Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 30 min Total Time: 40 min Instructions:

  • 1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, add ground beef, onions, garlic, salt, pepper, worcestershire sauce, and ketchup. Break the beef up to incorporate the seasonings. the seasonings and cook until brown. 5-7 minutes
  • 2. Pour in the beef broth and one cup of water. Bring to a simmer
  • 3. Add the pasta and simmer for 20 minutes or until the pasta is cooked throughout and the broth has cooked won, stirring occasionally.
  • 4. Pour the milk and cheese. Stir till the cheese has melted
  • 5. Serve hot and enjoy!

    • Chili Beef Noodles

    Serving Size: 4 people Ingredients:

  • 9 oz of beef
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp chinese 5 spice
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 red chili
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 9 oz of cooked noodles

    • Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 10 min Total Time: 20 min Instructions:

  • 1. Fry the beef for a couple minutes with the salt, pepper, and 5 spice. Take out and set aside.
  • 2. In the same pan, fry the garlic and red chili for a few minutes before stirring in the chili sauce and soy sauce
  • 3. Add the beef and noodles. Mix together for another couple of minutes
  • 4. Serve hot and enjoy!
